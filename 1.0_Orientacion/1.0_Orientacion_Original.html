1. Fill out a profile, share goals, and add a picture.

Start by filling out your profile . You'll notice that some of the information from your application has been automatically transferred to your profile but there are a few additional fields. We've added a space in your profile to share three learning goals you have for yourself . We'll revisit these goals at the end of the course. Also be sure to upload a picture of yourself!


2. Introduce yourself to the class

Head to the introduction thread and say hello to your classmates. Tell everyone a bit about yourself and what interests you about gender data!

Join the discussion!


3. Adjust your time zone

All course events are listed in Eastern Standard Time (EDT, GMT-4). ​Go to settings and adjust your timezone to your location.
